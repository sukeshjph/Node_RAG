Quarterly Report: Q2 2025

Revenue grew 12% year-on-year, reaching £8.5 billion, driven by strong performance in wealth management and digital banking services.

Key highlights:
- Net profit margin improved to 18%.
- Non-performing loans declined to 2.1%.
- Assets under management increased by £2.3 billion.

Future Outlook:
The bank expects continued growth in retail deposits and has announced a £500 million share buyback program for the next fiscal year.
